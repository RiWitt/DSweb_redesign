About the company

Experience that goes back more than a decade.

Alcasys DS builds on the team and know-how that spent more than ten years developing communication and information solutions for industry and transport as part of ALCASYS. Since 2025 we have operated as an independent company — Alcasys DS, s. r. o. — with our own identity, but with the same people, experience, and partnerships our customers have relied on all along.

What we do

The focus behind our work

Behind the experience we offer today is years of work on projects for carriers and operators of critical infrastructure. Becoming independent lets us focus exclusively on this specialization — without compromise, and with full accountability for every solution we deliver. Below is an overview of the three areas where we put this focus into practice.

Passenger Information Systems PIS
WiFi, camera systems, passenger counting, and information displays — one platform from boarding to the end of the journey.
Critical Infrastructure
Transport networks for operational control, plus enterprise LAN and WAN networks that cannot go down.
Cybersecurity
More than 10 years of experience as a certified Fortinet Advanced Partner specializing in Secure Networking LAN.
